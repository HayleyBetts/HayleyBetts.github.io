EGYPT 3100-332 B.C.
Egypt is a digital archive documenting the rich culture and history of Ancient Egypt. One of the world’s most prominent civilisations lasting 30 centuries from its unification around 3100 B.C. to its conquest by Alexander the Great in 332 B.C. Their long history and reign have led to a vast collection of archaeological discoveries, for thousands of years the Egyptians crafted some of the world’s most beautiful artefacts. However, there is still so much unknown about their culture which makes the artefacts and information that is known so significant. The main sources of information come the many monuments, objects and artefacts that have been recovered.
